Our Story

Aura Family Galore was established as the first ever Family Health & Nutraceutical shop in Kandy, with the vision to provide high-quality medical equipment and nutritional supplements to improve the quality of life for our customers.

As the largest retailer and wholesaler in the region, we pride ourselves on offering a comprehensive range of products from trusted brands at competitive prices.

Our dedicated team is committed to providing exceptional customer service and expert advice to help you find the right products for your specific needs.
